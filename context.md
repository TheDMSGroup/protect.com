# Landing Page Context: Protect.com Demand Gen / PMax LP

## Purpose
This document provides structured context for an AI assisting with the design, copy, or implementation of a **Protect.com landing page** modeled after **The Zebra’s high-performing LP format**.

The goal is to test a conversion-focused landing page for **demand generation / Performance Max traffic**, leveraging proven insurance comparison UX patterns while aligning with Protect.com’s brand values of **protection, safety, and trust**.

---

## Reference LP
**Source Inspiration:** The Zebra – Car Insurance Comparison Landing Page  
**Key Attributes:**
- Stark black & white layout
- Floating quote cards
- Data-driven trust signals
- Clear anti-spam messaging
- Strong social proof (Trustpilot)

---

## Brand Identity Shift

### The Zebra
- Color palette: Black / White / Zebra Blue
- Tone: Playful, data-heavy, comparison-focused

### Protect.com (Adapted)
- Core message: **Protection + Safety + Smart Savings**
- Emotional framing: Confidence, trust, peace of mind

**Recommended Color Palette**
- Deep Navy Blue: `#001f3f` (trust, authority)
- White: `#ffffff` (clarity, cleanliness)
- Emerald Green (CTA / savings): `#2ecc71`
- Accent Blue (icons / highlights): Brand-aligned Protect blue

## Page Structure & Section-by-Section Adaptation

### Header

- Phone number (right)

**Protect.com Adaptation**

- “Call a Specialist: [Phone Number]” (right)

### Hero Section

**Headline**
- Zebra:  
  *Your One-Stop Shop For Car Insurance Comparison*
- Protect.com:  
  **The Smarter Way to Protect Your Car and Your Wallet**

**Subheadline**
- *Save Over $900 Per Year by Comparing*
- Keep identical savings claim for continuity and performance parity.

**Primary CTA**
- Zebra: “Compare Now”

- Required

This copy must appear below every ZIP entry.

## Key Design Elements to Replicate

### Floating Quote Cards
- Use `box-shadow`
- Slight `translateY` animation
- Cards should feel layered and “alive”

---

### Anti-Spam Messaging (High Priority)
This messaging must appear:
- Under hero ZIP input
- Under footer ZIP input

It is a **major conversion driver** in insurance verticals.

---

### Visual Rhythm
- Light → Dark → Light page flow
- Clear section separation
- Strong headline hierarchy

---

## Implementation Notes (Optional Framework Guidance)

- Mobile-first layout
- Sticky CTA on smaller screens
- Avoid navigation distractions
- Page exists to convert, not educate broadly

---

## Primary Goal
Maximize **ZIP submits** for demand gen / PMax traffic while maintaining:
- Trust
- Clarity
- Low friction
- High perceived safety

This LP should feel:
> “Simple, safe, and obviously worth trying.”
